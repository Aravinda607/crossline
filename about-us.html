Welcome to Crossline - Your Gateway to the Exquisite Treasures of Sri Lanka


Asians have been known for their wonderful flavors of spices, silks, gems, and oriental crafts since ancient times. Ceylon enhances the Asian flavor with the best tea and coffee flavors and the finest spices in the world.

The Crossline story began by supplying goods that maintain pure Sri Lankan flavor. We promise to deliver the genuine, high-quality, transparent, and flavorful essence of Asia to your palm.

OUR TRADEMARK BELONGS TO THE UNIQUE ISLAND OF SRI LANKA

   At Crossline, our trademark proudly belongs to the unique island of Sri Lanka. With its former name, Ceylon, being synonymous with the finest spices, crafts, tea, and coffee, we draw inspiration from our ancient heritage to forge a new international platform that embodies principles of equity, transparency, and traceability.

   We are driven by a deep sense of pride in our commitment to delivering sustainably-sourced, pure Sri Lankan products. The island of Sri Lanka, with its lush landscapes and diverse ecosystems, provides the perfect backdrop for our quest to bring you the very best.

That's why we have established an international platform that is not only equitable but also transparent and traceable. We want you to experience the journey of our products, from the moment they are lovingly grown and crafted to the moment they reach your hands.

Discover our exquisite crafts that reflect the skill and artistry of our talented artisans. Delight in the aromatic charm of our tea and coffee, meticulously cultivated to capture the essence of our land.

OUR COMMITMENT TO SUSTAINABILITY OUR TRADEMARK BELONGS TO THE UNIQUE ISLAND OF SRI LANKA

   At Tropical Island, we hold a steadfast commitment to sustainability, guided by the knowledge that our trademark belongs to the unique island of Sri Lanka. For centuries, the spice industry of Sri Lanka has thrived on sustainable agricultural and sourcing practices, forming the very essence of Ceylon Spices' identity.

   Preserving this promise and tradition is at the heart of our mission. We understand the profound impact our actions have on the environment, local communities, and the future generations to come. With great responsibility, we strive to ensure that our practices not only protect but enhance the natural ecosystems that nurture our precious spices.

   From the fertile soils of our plantations to the hands that carefully cultivate and harvest each spice, sustainability is ingrained in every step of our process. We prioritize responsible farming methods that prioritize biodiversity, conservation, and the well-being of our farmers. OUR PRODUCTS

Cinnamon

Exquisite Ceylon spice known for its exceptional flavor and numerous health benefits. Elevate your dishes with this aromatic and versatile ingredient.

Pepper

: Premium-quality pepper sourced from the finest farms. Add a bold and distinct flavor to your culinary creations with this essential spice.

Clove

Warm and aromatic spice that adds a touch of sweetness and complexity to both sweet and savory dishes. Experience its rich and comforting flavor..

Cardamom

Exotic and fragrant spice with a unique blend of sweet and savory notes. Enhance your recipes with the captivating taste of cardamom.

Nutmeg & Mace

Aromatic duo with warm and slightly sweet flavors. Sprinkle this flavorful combination to add depth and richness to your culinary masterpieces.

Ceylon Cinnamon Oil

: Pure and potent oil extracted from Ceylon cinnamon. Discover its rich aroma and potential health benefits for culinary and wellness applications.

Clove Essential Oil

Invigorating and versatile essential oil derived from cloves. Enjoy its soothing aroma and explore its various uses in aromatherapy and personal care.

Citronella Oil

Refreshing and uplifting oil known for its insect-repelling properties. Harness its invigorating scent to create a pleasant and bug-free environment.

Tea

Handpicked and expertly crafted teas that captivate your senses. From delicate and floral to robust and bold, experience the diverse flavors of our premium tea selection.

Coffee

Finest coffee beans carefully selected and expertly roasted to perfection. Awaken your senses with the rich and nuanced flavors of our exceptional coffee.

Cane Products

Naturally sweet and flavorful products made from pure cane. Indulge in the richness of jaggery, molasses, and other cane-derived delights.

Pottery

Handcrafted pottery showcasing the artistic heritage of Sri Lanka. Each piece reflects the skill and passion of our artisans, adding elegance to your space.
